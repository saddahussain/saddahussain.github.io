Experience

Android Developer

Upgenics International (Oct 2018 - April 2019)

Roles and Responsibilities:

Upgenics International provided me the opportunity to develop a mobile hardware diagnostic SDK known as MobileT SDK. After developing the SDK, I started developing MobileT App which utilizes MobileT SDK to test hardware of Android devices. Moreover, I was the part of the team which was responsible for development and optimization of PhoneCheck app, which is a USA based product.

I participated in the development of following Apps:

  • 1Receipt™ is a paper receipt replacement solution, this app lets you have all of your receipts in one place. This way you don’t need to fear or look for receipts later, and it also saves your wallet space from being overcrowded with little pieces of paper flying around. I developed this app in Xamarin.Android and wrote the backend for Xamarin.IOS in C#.
  • Smart Attendance is an in-house application which is used to mark attendance, apply for leave, chat with the company and receive company wide notifications. I added the reports feature in the app to allow the employees to check their total present days, leave days, absent days, late arrivals, their total working and break hours and their average check in time.
  • NFC Provisioning App is built on android-NfcProvisioning to use NFC to provision factory reset devices with a device owner. Device owner is a specialized type of device administrator that can control device security and configuration. The primary purpose of the app was to bypass the setup wizard of Android devices. This app also supports provisioning using QR and NFC Tags.
  • Android DPC is built on android-testdpc. This app serves as a sample Device Policy Controller that we customized to gain access to developer options and perform other licensing operations.
  • MobileT SDK is a mobile hardware diagnostic SDK developed completely in Kotlin. I developed manager classes for all the tests. User can perform all the tests using these managers and can get test results using simple callbacks.
  • MobileT App is a mobile hardware testing app which utilizes MobileT SDK to test hardware of Android devices.
  • PhoneCheck is the most comprehensive diagnostic solution in the industry. Built from the ground up to cover over 60 possible defects, it removes potential human errors making PhoneCheck the most efficient testing product available.
  • PhoneCheck Consumer is the light version of PhoneCheck with less tests and functionality which was developed to serve the needs of their clients. eBay was one of their clients.

Tools and Technologies:

  • Java, Kotlin & C#.
  • Android Studio, Visual Studio, SourceTree, Trello & JIRA
