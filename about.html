About Me

I'm a Senior Android Developer with 8 years of experience building mobile applications from concept to Play Store release. I've worked across B2B, B2C, and C2C domains — including booking, healthcare, VPN, virtual fitness, marketplaces, and hardware-integrated self-service kiosks — and led teams of 10+ engineers delivering production-grade apps at scale.

Currently working as a Top Rated Freelance Android Engineer on Upwork (100% Job Success) across 8 completed projects. Recent work includes an in-store kiosk for BuyBackX enabling instant device quotes and cash buybacks, and a cloud-based diagnostics platform for Cellular Optics covering automated testing, IMEI checks, data erasure, and battery health analysis.

Previously at Xenex Media (Australia) for nearly 6 years, I shipped apps for mood, sleep, and activity tracking with LLM integration (OpenAI GPT-4o Mini, Groq LLaMA 3.1), Stripe payments, biometric authentication, AES encryption, and white-label multi-language support. Published titles include SwapCo, BoroMe, FreshFeelz, and GoCycle.

I build with Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, MVVM, and Clean Architecture — and care deeply about security, performance, and code that's maintainable long after launch.
