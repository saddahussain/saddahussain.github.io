Intro

My name is Sadda Hussain. I live and work in Sialkot, Pakistan. My name is listed in top 200 GitHub contributors from Pakistan (https://commits.top/pakistan.html). I develop Android apps, but I do a bit of dabbling here and there. I'm a developer with a strong entrepreneurial bent. I love helping companies grow, develop, and disrupt the state of things. The nature of everything is change. You don't adapt, you die, and I don't intend on getting out of the game anytime soon.

My background in mobile app development gives me a unique perspective on building Android apps pixel perfect. I can see the whole picture and I can explain it in the simplest terms possible. I pull out all the techno-babble, because that's not how I think about it. Why explain it that way? My unique perspective also allows me to be the best devil's advocate out there. I relish it. So many people ask how, what, where, and when, but they forget to ask why. Why would someone use this? Why do you think they behave this way? Why is this the best solution to this problem? It's always best to get a poor idea out of the way, so a great one can take root.

Technology and software in particular have the amazing ability to change the world for the better. It flattens the playing field, creates new insights, and opens doors we never knew were there. I'm building that future and making ideas a reality. Beyond being up to my elbows in code and logic, I enjoy spending time with my family and friends, I also love to watch stand up comedy and debates on abstract topics like soul, peace and happiness etc.
